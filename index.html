About Me

I am a tenure track associate professor at School of Data Science, Fudan University. I obtained my PhD in Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford. Before joining Fudan, I have been postdoctoral research scholars in HKUST and UC Davis for three years.

Research Interests

I am broadly interested in computational problems arising from signal processing, machine learning and data science, with an emphasis on the design and analysis of efficient numerical algorithms via tools from numerical linear algebra, optimization, probability and statistics.

Research areas of current focus include high dimensional structured signal processing, reinforcement learning, and mathematical foundations of data science. For those who are interested in working with me, feel free to drop me an email.
